EMERGENCY CARE

Immediate Medical Attention, Anytime.

If you require urgent care, please visit our Emergency Center as soon as you can. Just bring any details of prior visits or paperwork, and our team will prioritize your treatment.

Emergency Arrival Instructions

Please follow these steps to save time:

  • Arrive at our Emergency Center located at ground floor.
  • Inform our reception staff immediately about the urgency of your situation.
  • Our priority team will assist you upon arrival and guide you to the appropriate care area.
