WELCOME TO STELLAR HEALTH

A Multi-speciality Hospital and a beacon of exceptional healthcare in the heart of Vidarbha and Central India.

At Stellar Health, we bring together decades of deep clinical expertise and cutting-edge, groundbreaking innovation with a steadfast commitment to a patient-first philosophy. Every facet of our care is thoughtfully designed to ensure that you receive not only the highest standard of treatment but also an experience that is deeply personalized and compassionate. Our multidisciplinary team works in harmony to customize solutions that cater to your unique health needs, blending time-honored medical wisdom with the latest advances in technology. At Stellar Hospital, each moment redefines healthcare excellence and transforms your journey to wellness into a truly exceptional experience.

Founded by a visionary team of esteemed medical professionals alongside a dedicated healthcare entrepreneur, our institution stands as a testament to innovation and commitment in the world of healthcare. We blend deep clinical expertise with groundbreaking research to deliver world-class care that is not only clinically advanced but also infused with genuine compassion and precision that borders on surgical perfection. Every decision we make is driven by an unwavering dedication to ensuring your health and well-being.

At Stellar Health, we pride ourselves on assembling a distinguished roster of experts whose remarkable achievements and relentless drive place them at the very forefront of medical excellence. Each team member is a leader in their respective specialty, working collaboratively to elevate the standard of patient care while cultivating a culture of continuous innovation, integrity, and holistic healing.

Dr. Nitin Shinde – Renowned as an esteemed Infectious Disease Specialist, Dr. Shinde leverages decades of experience combined with cutting-edge diagnostic techniques to address and manage complex infections. His meticulous approach ensures that every patient’s unique condition is thoroughly evaluated and treated with tailored, expert care that harmonizes the latest scientific advancements with deeply compassionate support.

Dr. Nishant Deshpande – As a renowned Nephrologist, Dr. Deshpande is dedicated to advancing kidney health through a blend of innovative treatments and empathetic care. His approach is anchored in the latest clinical research and practice, ensuring that each therapeutic intervention is both effective and considerate, providing hope and healing to patients facing renal challenges.

Dr. Tushar Patil – Widely recognized as a distinguished Neurologist, Dr. Patil offers an in-depth understanding of neurological conditions spanning a broad spectrum of disorders. Employing state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic techniques, he supports and enhances patient recovery by crafting treatment plans that are not only advanced but also meticulously tailored to address the unique needs of each individual.

Dr. Rashmi Shinde – With extensive experience as a seasoned Obstetrician & Gynaecologist, Dr. Rashmi Shinde specializes in managing high-risk pregnancies and pioneering assisted reproductive technologies. Her practice blends scientific expertise and heartfelt sensitivity, ensuring that expectant mothers and their families receive comprehensive, personalized care that safeguards both physical health and emotional well-being.

Supporting this extraordinary collection of medical talent is Mr. Shrikant Waghulkar, a dynamic healthcare entrepreneur whose innovative vision has been a catalyst for transformative change. His relentless dedication and strategic acumen have been instrumental in developing our state-of-the-art facility—designed meticulously to adapt to the diverse and evolving health needs of our vibrant community.
